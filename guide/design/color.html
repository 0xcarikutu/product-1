Color Scheme

Brand Colors

Our palette is centered around Datto Blue. Blue conveys security and trust, while also evoking feeling of tranquility and calm. Datto Blue should be present on every deliverable, even if it's just a fine line.

UI Status Colors

The main UI colors are used for action buttons, alert messages, and form elements. Input fields, for example, can get positive and negative feedback, whereas alert messages can have warning feedback.
